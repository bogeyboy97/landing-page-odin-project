My favorite One Piece Characters!

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat pirates! His unwavering tenacity is complemented by a strong case of ADHD.

Roronoa Zoro

The second member of the Straw Hat pirates. On a quest to be the worlds greatest swordsman. Can be a party pooper at times.

"Black Leg" Sanji

The fifth member of our crew and the ships 5 star cook. When it comes to the ladies, no one is more down bad than my boy.

"Cat Burglar" Nami

The third member of the crew. Nami doesnt have any special powers but she is a GOAT navigator and a certified baddie.
